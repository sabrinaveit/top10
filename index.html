Top 10 Most Spoken Languages

In the cards below, you will find the top 10 languages spoken worldwide. Each card includes the number of speakers worldwide, the genus, a button with audio on how to say “Hello, my name is” in that specific language, and a map displaying the places where that language is spoken. The maps show all the places, whether big or small, where that language is spoken at. The information for the maps was taken from the CIA World Factbook.
